    Introducing Broker

    Up to $10 Commission per Lot on Clients who enter into a Client Agreement with the Company

    IBs are partners who maintain business portfolios and receive monthly commission for all the clients and sub-IBs they refer to XM.

    Earn up to $10 per lot on referred clients and 10% on other IBs referred. All IBs also receive full access to the partners' internal Members Area, which provides advanced statistics of their performance.

    Web Affiliates

    Up to $10 Commission per Lot - Ideal for Website Owners

    We offer up to $10 per lot on referred clients and 10% on other IBs referred.

    Every time an XM Demo or Real Account is opened by a client who clicked on a banner or link from a Web Affiliate’s website, the new client is automatically added to their partner account.

    Register Here

    Local Representatives

    Set up your Own XM office with Our Help

    Individuals with the skills to manage a team, can setup their own local office with the help of XM.

    Local Representatives receive direct assistance from the XM Headquarters to implement a fully operational model which includes both start-up capital and assistance with overhead costs.
